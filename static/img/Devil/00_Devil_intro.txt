Devil
AGIO MP1000 Bluetooth Mic & Speaker

Devil is designed to be a fun tech gadget by employing microphone and speaker function. With the bluetooth connection with smart phone, people can hold it and enjoy singing Karaoke songs streaming online. Bring AGIO Plus Devil with you to enjoy Karaoke whenever, wherever you are. The additional features for example SOS alarm, cute style, safe material, light and handy design make AGIO Plus Devil also a fabulous gift for children.
